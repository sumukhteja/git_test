The only difference in the ordered and unordered lists are the type of bullets used. Ordered list has numbered bullets signifying there is a sequence/order to be followed. Whereas the unordered list follows a simple bullet signifying there is no sequence/order.

The Ordered list starts and ends with ol element:

  1. The fist thing item in the list starts and ends with li inside the ol
  2. So does the second item

The Unordered list starts and ends with ul element:

  • This is the first item in the list, starts and ends with li
  • Likewise, this is the second item in the list
